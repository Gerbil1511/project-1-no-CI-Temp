Book a talk with a Bright Futures Ambassador
or
Volunteer to be a Bright Futures Ambassador

To enhance your efforts in teaching diversity and inclusion, you can book a talk with a Bright Futures ambassador. Our Bright Futures ambassadors are dedicated to educating children in schools about diversity and inclusion in a creative and engaging way. When you book a talk, an ambassador will visit your school to deliver an interactive and inspiring session tailored to the Key Stage your child is at. These talks use stories, games, and activities to help children understand and appreciate the importance of diversity, inclusion, and social justice.
By becoming a Bright Futures Ambassador, you will undergo comprehensive training designed to equip you with the skills and confidence to deliver these impactful talks. The training includes modules on effective communication, group facilitation, and the presentation of key concepts related to diversity and inclusion aimed at young children. You will also receive support materials, such as presentation guides and resources, to help you prepare and deliver engaging sessions. Additionally, you may participate in practice sessions and receive feedback to ensure you are well-prepared to educate and inspire the children. By becoming an ambassador, you will be part of a network of dedicated individuals committed to promoting diversity, inclusion, and positive social change in a fun and educational manner.
